Tagging Strategy Tool

A clean tag system keeps your emails relevant for years. Plan yours with the rule of "behaviour, source, interest".

Use only 3 tag families — anything more becomes chaos:
  • Source — where they came from (lead-magnet-name, ad, partner)
  • Interest — what topics they click on
  • Behaviour — what they did (buyer, opened-launch, cold-90d)
