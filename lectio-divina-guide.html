Lectio Divina Guide

A guided four-step practice of sacred reading — read, reflect, respond, rest.

Read it slowly, twice. Which word or phrase shimmers, or chooses you?
Sit with that word. What does it stir in you? Why this, today?
Speak honestly from the heart. Turn your reflection into a prayer.
Let go of words. Simply rest in stillness. Note anything afterward.
