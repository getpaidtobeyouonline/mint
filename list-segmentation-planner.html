List Segmentation Planner

Stop blasting everyone the same email. Plan the segments that lift opens, clicks, and sales.

High-ROI segments to consider:
  • Engagement: active vs. cold (protects deliverability)
  • Lead source: which freebie / where they came from
  • Buyer vs. non-buyer: never pitch buyers the thing they own
  • Interest / topic: what they clicked or told you
  • Stage: new subscriber vs. long-time
