Dyslexia Friendly Study Planner

Plan study in short, clear chunks — with methods that play to dyslexic strengths.

Use your ears
Audiobooks, text-to-speech and watching videos count as real study.
Short bursts
Study for 15-20 minutes, then take a proper break. Little and often wins.
Show it, do not just read it
Mind maps, diagrams and colour beat dense blocks of text.
Say it out loud
Explaining a topic aloud locks it in better than re-reading.
One thing at a time
One short task per line. Finishing small tasks builds momentum.
